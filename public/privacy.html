We built Campus Chat to make it easy to communicate and collaborate with other students at your college. Your privacy and security is our top priority.

Campus Chat ("Campus Chat") owns and runs various websites, including but not limited to CampusCh.at (the "Websites"), through which it offers various services and software (the Websites, services and software are referred to as the "Services").

Campus Chat respects the privacy of its users. This document explains how Campus Chat will use the information that you provide to us via the Services. Campus Chat may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If Campus Chat does so, the revised Privacy Policy will be posted on the Websites.

1. Information collected

When you register for Campus Chat we may ask for information such as your name and email address. Campus Chat uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
  • It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if Campus Chat is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Campus Chat will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

2. Security

Campus Chat takes appropriate precautions to protect our users' information. Your information is located on secure servers.

3. Storage, Disclosure and Use of Your Communications

By using Campus Chat for chatting, you consent to the of your chat history on Campus Chat application.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer's hard drive by our servers to identify your computer to our servers. No personally identifiable information stored in the Campus Chat cookies will be shared with outside companies for any purpose without your permission. We use cookies to record session information.

5. Data storage

When you use the file transfer feature of Campus Chat, the file that you transfer is copied and retained on the servers of our third party service provider after the transfer. Campus Chat does not access or uses these files for any purpose, but you should be aware that these files are not encrypted and that they could be disclosed in legal proceedings or investigations.

DISCLOSURE

Campus Chat may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service. It may be necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected or actual illegal activities, including without limitation, fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, or as otherwise permitted or required by law.

CHANGES

Campus Chat may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address on your Campus Chat account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
